About Tugwemo

Rwanda's Premier Social Connection Platform

Tugwemo is Rwanda's leading platform for anonymous video chatting and meaningful connections. Born from the spirit of "Tugwemo" - meaning "connect" in Kinyarwanda - we bridge the gap between technology and authentic human relationships.

Whether you're seeking new friendships, romantic connections, or simply engaging conversations, Tugwemo provides a safe, inclusive space where Rwandans and people worldwide can meet, share stories, and build lasting relationships.
